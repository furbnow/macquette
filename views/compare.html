Comparison tables

Only changes made by applying a measure are taken into account for the comparison. When an scenario is created as a copy of another one different than the Master, the changes are kept but not as measures. For example:

  • You apply EWI to a wall as a measure in Scenario 1
  • You create Scenario 2 as a copy of Scenario 1
Then that wall in Scenario 2 will still have EWI but the comparison table for scenario 2 will not show the difference with the Master scenario as the change is not considered a measure anymore.
